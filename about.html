About Us

Ernie and Flo are two artistic brothers looking to express their creativity in their own respective ways. From poetry, to mangas, to hip-hop and indie music, they do it all and refuse to be put in a box.

Ernie And Flo Creations is an answer to the question : "Who is crazy enough to buy into such an eclectic proposal?"
